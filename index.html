The Stroop Effect

The Stroop Effect is the difference in reaction time when presented with congruent and incongruent stimuli. For example, when presented with the first example above, the time it takes to recognize the color of the font should be less than the time it takes for the second example.

Instructions

You will be presented with a three second countdown, then word will appear. You must identify the color of the font, then select the color from the choices at the bottom of the screen.
